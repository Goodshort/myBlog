Aquaponics

This has been an ongoing project for a while now. I always loved fish, I had aquariums most of my life. Step by step I upgraded, bigger tanks, different types of fish, adding live plants in the tank.
But one of the issue with fish tank, is that you have to perform water changes weekly, this removes the nitrate that built-up. Adding live plants in the tank helps, but not enough. To recreate a perfect ecosystem, you have to add ground plants. These plants will absord the nitrate and clean up the water. So I am now growing some mint, salad, flowers. All of that out of my fish poop.
If you find this interesting, go ahead and google it! There are people have much bigger setup and not going for groceries anymore.

Date: May 2018 - June 2019
