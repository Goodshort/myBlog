Migration from G Suite to Office365

A friend of mine has his own busines with 3 different shop location. He has been using G Suite since the beggining but they are heavy users of the Microsoft Office Suite and then bought an Office 365 Business license. We had a talk at some point regarding that and we realized that there is no point to use G Suite only for emails and then use everything else with the office 365 license.

Home automation

When I bought my first raspberry Pi, I then started to do things seriously with Homebridge. I now have smart plugs, thermometers, motion sensors... They are all fully automated, and can be used either by voice or through the Home app on my iPhone/Mac.
